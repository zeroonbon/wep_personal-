Minimalist Design

Minimalist design focuses on the essentials, reducing visual clutter and displaying only the most important information. It uses lots of white space, simple typography, and neutral colors

Responsive Design

Responsive design allows a website to adapt to a variety of screen sizes, from desktop to mobile devices. Layouts and design elements such as images and text adjust to fit both small and large screens.

Flat Design

Flat design uses simple design elements,such as flat colors without shadows or 3D effects.This approach focuses on function over form, using clean, easy-to-understand UI elements.

Parallax Scrolling

This design utilizes the parallax effect,where the background moves more slowly than the foreground elements as the user scrolls through the page.This effect creates the illusion of depth and interactivity.
